Donation Rules & Requirements

Please read the following guidelines carefully before registering as a donor.

General Requirements

  • Donor must be between 18 to 65 years of age.
  • Should weigh at least 50 kg.
  • Must be in good health and free from chronic infections.
  • No history of major communicable diseases (HIV, Hepatitis, etc.).

Blood Donation Rules

  • Hemoglobin test: 12.5 g/dL (women), 13.0 g/dL (men).
  • Minimum gap of 3 months between two donations.
  • Normal hemoglobin level is required.
  • No alcohol or drugs 24 hours before donation.
  • Donor should not be fasting at the time of donation.

Organ Donation Rules

  • Donor must provide legal consent (self or family in case of brain death).
  • Must register officially with a recognized organization.
  • No transmissible diseases at the time of donation.
  • All donations must comply with Government & Medical Council regulations.
